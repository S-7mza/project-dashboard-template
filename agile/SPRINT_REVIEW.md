Sprint Review Template

Purpose
- Demonstrate the sprint increment to stakeholders, gather feedback, and validate acceptance.

When
- End of sprint (timebox: 60 minutes for a 2-week sprint; adjust for team size).

Attendees
- Product Owner, Development Team, Scrum Master, Stakeholders, Data Owners, UX/Design as required.

Preparation
- Ensure demo environment is populated with the latest sprint release (staging or demo sandbox).
- Prepare brief walkthroughs for each completed story.

Agenda
1. Opening & Sprint Goal recap (5 minutes).
2. Demo: Walk through completed stories and features (30–40 minutes).
3. Feedback & Q&A: Collect stakeholder feedback and clarify next steps (15–20 minutes).
4. Metrics & Health: Share relevant metrics (velocity, burndown, incidents) if appropriate (5–10 minutes).
5. Review Backlog Impact: PO notes potential reprioritization based on feedback.

Outputs
- Stakeholder feedback recorded in meeting minutes; new backlog items or adjustments created.
- Acceptance or follow-up actions recorded in `templates/DECISION_LOG.md`.

Notes
- Focus on working software and tangible outcomes. Keep slides minimal and use live demo where possible.
