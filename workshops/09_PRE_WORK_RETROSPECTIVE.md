Retrospective — Pre-work Checklist

Send 2–3 working days before the session.

Required inputs
- Collect feedback notes, major blockers, bug list and enhancement backlog.
- Usage metrics and key incidents since launch.
- Short pre-survey sent to participants to surface priorities.

Required attendees
- PM, BI lead, representatives from data, ETL, ops and product.

Attachments to include in invite
- Action log extract, bug/issue list, usage metrics report

Goals for the session
- Capture lessons learned and prioritize improvements.
- Create action items and owners for continuous improvement.

Post-meeting outputs
- Retrospective notes, prioritized backlog, assigned owners and dates.
