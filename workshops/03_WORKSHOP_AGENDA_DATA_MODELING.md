Data Modeling / Semantic Layer — Agenda (detailed)

Duration: 90–120 minutes
Attendees: BI lead, data modeler, ETL engineer, data owner, product owner

1. Recap of Data Discovery outputs (15 min)
- Confirm canonical sources, sample counts and known data quality issues.

2. Define grain and canonical entities (30 min)
- Agree on fact tables, dimensions, primary keys and slowly changing dimension strategy.

3. Semantic definitions & business logic (30 min)
- Map KPI definitions to model fields and define calculation rules.

4. Performance considerations (15 min)
- Partitioning, denormalization trade-offs, pre-aggregation needs.

5. Actions & owners (10 min)
- Assign modeling, dictionary updates, and ETL inputs.

Outputs
- Model diagram, canonical table definitions, updated `deliverables/KPI_SPEC.md` mappings and action log entries.
