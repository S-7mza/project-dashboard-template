Duration: 60 minutes
Attendees: Security/Compliance, Data Owner, PM, BI Lead, Legal (if needed)

1. Data classification review (15 min)
- Identify PII, confidential or restricted fields, and residency constraints.

2. Access controls and least-privilege (15 min)
- How users and services will authenticate and who approves access.

3. Masking / anonymization strategy (10 min)
- Discuss techniques for PII handling, tokenization, or aggregation.

4. Compliance requirements (10 min)
- Retention, audit logging, and any legal/regulatory constraints.

5. Actions & sign-offs (10 min)
- Assign owners for policy enforcement, audit steps, and monitoring rules.

Outputs
- Data classification register (update `docs/DATA_GOVERNANCE.md`), masking rules, and compliance action items.
